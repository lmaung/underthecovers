About this App

What is Under the Cover Series?

Under the Cover is a series of Modern UI applications built on Windows® 8 to show deverlopers how certain functions are programmed with clearly documented samples codes.

The application series is designed for beginners who are trying to get into the development. Although the series is aimed at introductory level, basic knowledge of programming is required.

How can I get the source?

The pre-compiled version of the applications can be downloaded from GitHub.
A free version of Visual Studio (Express) can be downloaded from Microsoft.

Who made this application?

His mobile applications have been featured on technology sites such as engadget, gizmodo, and pocket now. He has also designed and created programmable microcontrollers and robots from the ground up. He is one of the Hero developers for Microsoft Generation App program. Lwin has also help create Windows 8 certification exams for Microsoft.
