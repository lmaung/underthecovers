About this App

What is Under the Cover Series?

Under the Cover is a series of Modern UI applications built on Windows® 8 to show deverlopers how certain functions are programmed with documented samples source codes. The application series is designed for beginners who are trying to get into the development. Although the series is aimed at introductory level, a basic knowledge of programming is required.

Who made this application?

The application series is created by Lwin Maung. Lwin Maung is a (mobile) developer and an expert on mechatronics with over 9 years of consulting experience. His mobile applications have been featured on technology sites such as engadget, gizmodo, and pocket now. He has also designed and created programmable microcontrollers and robots from the ground up. He is one of the Hero Developers for Microsoft Generation App program. Lwin has also help create Windows 8 certification exams for Microsoft. He has co-authored certification study guide for ASP.NET MVC4 exam. You can find Lwin speaking at technical conferences across the country.
